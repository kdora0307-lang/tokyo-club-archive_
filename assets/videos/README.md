# Videos

Drop your video files here with these exact filenames:

- `usb.mp4` — plays when the USB is clicked
- `polaroid-1.mp4` — first video in the polaroid switcher
- `polaroid-2.mp4` — second video in the polaroid switcher
- `polaroid-3.mp4` — third video in the polaroid switcher

Recommended: MP4 with H.264 video + AAC audio for broadest browser support.
Until you add the files, clicking will show a placeholder with the expected filename.
